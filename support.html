Contact us

Email support@caw-academy.com — we aim to reply within a couple of working days. You can also use the Feedback form inside the app (Profile → Feedback), which reaches the same inbox.

Frequently asked

Is CAW Academy an official EASA product?

No. It is an independent study aid, not affiliated with or endorsed by EASA. It is aligned with the EASA Easy Access Rules but is not an official publication and not a substitute for the official regulations or your organisation’s procedures.

Which courses are available?

All 20 courses span the full airworthiness lifecycle, with no ads. We keep adding new lessons and refinements over time.

How is it licensed?

CAW Academy is licensed for organisations — your team gets all 20 courses under a single agreement, with a free trial to evaluate it first. Individual in-app purchases for private learners are planned for a later stage. To arrange a demonstration or a free trial, email support@caw-academy.com or use the contact form on our home page.

How do I reset my progress?

Profile → Overall progress → Reset. This clears read lessons, scores and reviews on the device.

Does the app work offline?

Yes — once installed, all course content works fully offline.
